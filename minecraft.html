instructions to play:)
  • Movement – W, A, S, & D keys
  • Jump – Spacebar
  • Crouch/Sneak – Hold Shift
  • Access inventory – E
  • Use the mouse to look around
  • F5 – cycle through different camera views
  • Break blocks or attack – left click
  • Run – Double tap W

Tips for beginners:)
  • Don’t bother getting a full set of wood tools
  • Find a cave first
  • Learn about your modded world
  • Adjust your settings
  • Bring a crafting table
  • Bring a bed
